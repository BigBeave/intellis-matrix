# INTELLIS MARKET MATRIX AI v5 📈

A supercharged, single-file "Matrix" trading dashboard designed for speed, sub-second momentum detection, and professional-grade market awareness.

## 🚀 Speed Features
- **Alpaca Matrix Scanner**: Sub-second price streaming via Alpaca Markets IEX (Free tier). Detects 2%+ price spikes/drops instantly.
- **Supercharged Squawk**: Integrated **Financial Juice** live audio feed to keep your ears on the market.
- **News Correlation**: Automatically fetches relevant news catalysts the moment a momentum alert triggers.
- **Matrix UI**: Specialized high-contrast dark theme optimized for active trade monitoring.
- **PWA for iPhone**: Full support for iOS "Add to Home Screen" with standalone windowing.

## ⚙️ Setup
1. **Finnhub Key**: (Free) Required for general ticker quotes and company news. [finnhub.io](https://finnhub.io)
2. **Alpaca Keys**: (Free) Required for sub-second momentum scanning. [alpaca.markets](https://alpaca.markets)
3. **Anthropic Key**: (Optional) For AI Analysis of charts and sentiment. [anthropic.com](https://anthropic.com)

## 📦 Deployment
Since this is a Progressive Web App (PWA), you can:
- **Local**: Open `index.html` or run `npm start`.
- **Hosting**: Drag and drop this folder into **GitHub Pages**, **Vercel**, or **Render**.
- **Mobile**: Open your hosted URL in iPhone Safari and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
